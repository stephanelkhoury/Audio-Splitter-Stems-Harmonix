About Us

About Harmonix

We're on a mission to democratize professional audio production, making it accessible to creators everywhere through cutting-edge AI technology.

Our Mission

At Harmonix, we believe that creative tools should be accessible to everyone. Whether you're a bedroom producer, a professional DJ, or a music educator, you deserve access to the same powerful audio separation technology used by major studios.

Founded in 2024, Harmonix combines state-of-the-art AI models with intuitive interfaces to deliver professional-grade stem separation, lyrics transcription, and audio analysis tools that work right in your browser.

We're committed to continuous innovation, constantly improving our algorithms to deliver the highest quality separations while maintaining the speed and accessibility our users depend on.
