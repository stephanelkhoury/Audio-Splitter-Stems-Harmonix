Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about stem separation and vocal removal.

What is stem separation and how does it work?

Stem separation is the process of isolating individual audio components (vocals, drums, bass, instruments) from a mixed song. Harmonix uses advanced AI neural networks trained on millions of songs to analyze spectral patterns and separate each element with studio-quality precision. Our technology is based on state-of-the-art models like Demucs, achieving 99% accuracy in most cases.

Is Harmonix really free to use?

Yes! Harmonix offers a generous free tier that allows you to process 3 songs per month with 4-stem separation. This is perfect for trying out the service and casual use. For more demanding needs, we offer affordable Creator and Studio plans with additional features and higher limits.

How accurate is the vocal removal?

Harmonix achieves 99% accuracy in vocal removal using state-of-the-art AI models. The separated stems maintain high audio quality suitable for professional use in music production, remixing, karaoke creation, and content creation. Results may vary depending on the complexity of the original mix.

What audio formats are supported?

Harmonix supports all popular audio formats including MP3, WAV, FLAC, M4A, OGG, AAC, and more. You can also paste YouTube URLs to process songs directly. Output stems are available in high-quality WAV (lossless) or MP3 format for easy sharing and use in any DAW or audio software.

Can I use Harmonix for karaoke?

Absolutely! Harmonix is perfect for creating karaoke tracks. Simply upload a song, and our AI will remove the vocals to create a clean instrumental. We also extract synced lyrics with word-level timing that display karaoke-style during playback. Export in LRC format for use with any karaoke software.

How long does processing take?

Most songs are processed in under 2 minutes. Processing time depends on the song length and current server load. Premium users get priority processing with faster queue times. You'll receive a notification when your stems are ready to download or stream in our built-in studio player.

What is the difference between 4-stem and 6-stem separation?

4-stem separation extracts: Vocals, Drums, Bass, and Other (instruments). 6-stem separation provides more granular control by also separating Guitar and Piano into their own tracks. The 6-stem model is available on paid plans and is ideal for musicians who want to isolate specific instruments for learning or remixing.

Is Harmonix better than Spleeter or LALAL.AI?

Harmonix uses the latest Demucs models which consistently outperform older technologies like Spleeter in blind listening tests. Compared to services like LALAL.AI, Harmonix offers competitive quality with additional features like synced lyrics extraction, a built-in mixing studio, and MIDI conversion.
