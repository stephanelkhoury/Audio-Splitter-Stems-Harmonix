FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! All plans are billed monthly with no long-term commitment. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings.

What payment methods?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex), PayPal, and bank transfers for Studio plans. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

What's the audio quality?

Our AI models produce studio-quality separations. Creator and Studio plans include lossless WAV/FLAC exports for professional use.

Do unused songs roll over?

Monthly song allocations don't roll over. However, all your processed songs remain accessible in your library indefinitely.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! New users get a 7-day free trial of Creator features. No credit card required to start. Experience the full power of Harmonix.

What about copyright?

You own all outputs from your own music. For copyrighted content, ensure you have rights to process it. We don't store or claim ownership.
